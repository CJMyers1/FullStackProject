How it works

From Recipe to Meal Plan in 4 quick steps

01

Pick how many meals a week

When you start the app it will ask how many meals a week you want to plan out of 6 12 and 18 meals a week.

02

Input your meals and recipes

Input the name of your meal, then choice to enter a recipe yourself or add via a URL.

03

Compile an ingredient list

Once done inputing your meals MealBuddy will take all the ingredients from each meal and show you how much you need. And if you have some you can lower the amount needed.

04

Load your shopping list

Thats all you have to do. MealBuddy does all the hard work for you, you can even add items to your grocery list if you need to.
